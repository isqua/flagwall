      How to download the image on a smartphone?
      Tap and hold on the image for a second or two until a menu appears, then select "Download image" or "Save image".
      What to do with the downloaded image?
      We made this app to set the wallpaper for a phone lock screen, but you can use the image as you want.
      What is the resolution of the picture?
      The same as your device screen.
      How to share my flag set with my friends?
      Just share the address of the current page as you usually share with friends. When you add a country, we update the page address.
